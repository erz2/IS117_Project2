"Helping You Get Your Axe Together"

Piero's Music was founded in 1952, and since then we've strived to provide our customers with the best possible service. We are proud to be a family owned and operated business, and as such, we hope to treat all of our customers as one of our own. Whether you need a new drum kit, need to fix an old guitar, or you want to learn how to play the bass, we aim to satisfy all of your musical needs. So stop on by and see us. You'll be glad you did.

Lessons

Piero's Music offers musical instruction for guitar, bass, drums, and piano. We offer both half hour and hour long sessions. Half hour sessions cost $13.50 per session. Hour long sessions cost $26.00 per session. Sessions are weekly, and all students must pay for sessions within a given month \ at the beginning of each month. Call 201-437-3220 to inquire about openings. In the case wherein there are no sessions available, we have a wait list that can reserve your spot until a session opens up.
