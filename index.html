Piero's Music was founded in 1952, and since then we've strived to provide our customers with the best possible service. We are proud to be a family owned and operated business, and as such, we hope to treat all of our customers as one of our own. Whether you need a new drum kit, need to fix an old guitar, or you want to learn how to play the bass, we aim to satisfy all of your musical needs. So stop on by and see us. You'll be glad you did.
